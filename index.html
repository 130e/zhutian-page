Zhutian Liu

I am a Ph.D student with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at University of California, Riverside. My advisor is Prof. Zhaowei Tan. Prior to UCR, I got my BS degree at UESTC.

In my PhD research, I mostly work with wireless network systems. My research focuses on enhancing cellular network performance, improving O-RAN system interoperability, and strengthening Wi-Fi security.

Recently, I am working on analyzing interactions between the transport and wireless link layers.
